## About FlowForge

FlowForge is a visual production planner for Satisfactory. It turns complex factory chains into a clear, interactive graph where you can connect recipes, adjust production rates, identify shortages, and understand how changes affect the rest of your factory.

### Highlights

- Visual node-based production planning
- Automatic recalculation across connected production branches
- Context-aware recipe suggestions
- Missing and excess resource indicators
- Editable production rates and machine counts
- Lockable and completable production steps
- Persistent plans stored in your browser
- Infinite canvas with pan and zoom controls
- Industrial interface designed for information-heavy factory planning

## Feedback and support

This repository is the public feedback and issue tracker for FlowForge. Use it to report bugs, request features, suggest improvements, and share usability feedback.

Before creating a new issue, please [search the existing issues](https://github.com/INVE-Studios/satisfactory-planner/issues). If a similar issue already exists, add your details or reaction there instead of creating a duplicate.

### Report a bug

[Create a bug report](https://github.com/INVE-Studios/satisfactory-planner/issues/new?labels=bug&title=%5BBug%5D%20) and include:

1. A clear description of the problem
2. The exact steps needed to reproduce it
3. What you expected to happen
4. What happened instead
5. Your browser and operating system
6. The recipes, resources, and connections involved
7. A screenshot or short recording, if possible

For calculation issues, include the machine count, expected rate, and displayed rate. Small reproducible examples help us investigate much faster.

### Suggest a feature

[Submit a feature request](https://github.com/INVE-Studios/satisfactory-planner/issues/new?labels=enhancement&title=%5BFeature%5D%20) and describe:

- The problem you are trying to solve
- How the proposed workflow could work
- Why it would improve real factory planning
- Any alternative solutions you have considered

Sketches, references, and practical examples are welcome. A clearly explained problem is more useful than a perfectly polished solution.

### Share general feedback

If something feels confusing, slow, difficult to discover, or visually unclear, [share your feedback](https://github.com/INVE-Studios/satisfactory-planner/issues/new?labels=feedback&title=%5BFeedback%5D%20).

Small observations matter. They often lead to the most meaningful product improvements.

## Issue guidelines

Please keep discussions constructive and focused on the product.

- Use one issue per bug or suggestion
- Choose a descriptive title
- Avoid duplicate reports
- Add screenshots only when they help explain the issue
- Do not post passwords, access tokens, personal information, or other sensitive data
- Remember that everything posted in this repository is publicly visible

Issues that do not contain enough information may be closed until additional details are available.

## Product status

FlowForge is under active development. Features, calculations, and interface details may evolve as the product grows and community feedback is incorporated.

Submitting an idea does not guarantee that it will be implemented, but every well-described report helps us understand what matters most to users.
